Trulisucheng

Real-time rice trading platform with blockchain support.

Industry: Trading platform, Blockchain

It is an online marketplace that brings together rice exporters, importers and inspection companies in one place to trade and commercialize rice in a more efficient, safe, transparent and efficient manner.Previously, we provided a client with some small blockchain related solutions and they decided to build a decentralized rice marketplace with us.

Services:

  • Extended team
  • Quality assurance
  • Web development

Technologies:

  • Python
  • Node JS
  • RoR
  • Angular

Necessity

Since there was no common platform that would unite all participants in the rice market under one roof, the idea was to create a holistic accounting platform that would become the preferred place for market participants to trade rice.

Focus

Our principle is to identify the right approach, the right technologies, and the right architecture based on the problems or pain points we need to solve. So, we identified the key pain points that the solution was supposed to solve.

Waste of time

There is a lot of time-consuming manual work everywhere: finding the right supplier, filling out all the necessary documents, etc. In addition, the physical movement of documents around the world leads to significant time delays.

Loss of money

It is difficult for market participants to keep track of how reasonable the price of a particular supplier is and to find profitable deals. In addition, documents need to be checked and matched manually, which can result in a high cost of human error.

High risks

Participants in the rice market send documents around the world by courier, which increases the risk of lost documents. At the same time, there is a risk of doing business with an unscrupulous partner, which can cost a pretty penny.

Goal

A digital platform that connects sellers and buyers by adding transparency, reliability, speed, and insightful analytics that help members easily find the best prices, the best partners, and the best conditions.

Digital entities that market participants use to interact.

Digital interfaces and process simulation sequences including PDF contract generator.

Blockchain-based digital system that immutably stores all information and history of interactions or transactions.

Digital keys issued by entitlement certificates.

Tokens.

Smart contracts on the blockchain.

Trust and transparency VS Security

We needed to balance the transparency offered by DLT with the need for security to prevent fraud and misuse of the platform.

Seamless digital transformation

We needed to design and develop a UX/UI that seamlessly encompasses all offline use cases for key market players. It should be intuitive, informative, and consistent with the client's branding.

Transparency, Security, Stability

To ensure transparency along with security, we have created a private blockchain based architecture with the ability for participants to host their own blockchain nodes. This approach provides 2 key results:

  • Absence of irrelevant participants
  • System immutability

To ensure platform stability from an architectural point of view, we chose to use an Azure cluster configuration with a load balancer.
